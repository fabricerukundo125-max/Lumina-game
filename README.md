Lumina

A relaxing puzzle game about guiding light through a forgotten world.

Players rotate mirrors, redirect beams, activate prisms, and illuminate crystals to restore light and reveal hidden beauty.

---

Vision

Lumina is designed around four core pillars:

- Relaxation
- Beauty
- Discovery
- Satisfaction

There are no timers, no combat, and no stressful mechanics. Every puzzle is intended to feel calm, elegant, and rewarding.

---

Core Gameplay

The player guides beams of light through a 7×7 puzzle grid.

Objects include:

Sources

Emit beams of light in a fixed direction.

Mirrors

Reflect beams and can be rotated by the player.

Prisms

Split white light into multiple colored beams.

Crystals

Targets that must be illuminated to complete the puzzle.

Walls

Block light and create routing challenges.

---

Puzzle Design Philosophy

Each puzzle should:

- Teach one concept at a time
- Be solvable without guessing
- Encourage experimentation
- Reward observation
- Increase difficulty gradually

The goal is understanding, not frustration.

---

Not Included In MVP

The following features are intentionally postponed:

- Multiplayer
- User accounts
- Chat systems
- AI-generated puzzles
- Community sharing
- Subscriptions
- Advanced world mechanics

The focus is delivering a polished and enjoyable puzzle experience first.

---

Long-Term Vision

As players restore light across the world, new environments awaken:

- Forests
- Oceans
- Mountains
- Mist realms
- Celestial archives

The final experience should feel less like solving puzzles and more like restoring a living world through light.

---

Development Philosophy

Build small.

Ship early.

Test with real players.

Improve based on observation.

Every feature must strengthen at least one of the four core pillars:

- Relaxation
- Beauty
- Discovery
- Satisfaction

If it does not, it should be removed.
